OUR STORY

A small shop with big intentions.

Maple & Bean started in 2014 as a four-seat espresso bar on a quiet corner of Linden Street. A decade later, it's still small just a little warmer, a little better dialed-in, and a lot more loved.

Source carefully

We work directly with three importers who pay growers above Fair Trade premiums.

Roast in small batches

Five-kilo drops on a vintage Probat. Tasted, scored, and dialed each morning.

Serve with care

Every drink leaves the bar the way we'd want to drink it. No shortcuts.

“The best part of opening a coffee shop isn't the coffee. It's the same faces, every morning, becoming friends.”
— Nora & James, founders
